Food Festival

Professional Skills Module Conducted by Mrs. Ishari Ravihari

The Professional Skills module, led by the esteemed Mrs. Ishari Ravihari, offers an insightful and engaging experience aimed at empowering individuals with the essential skills for success in today's fast-paced professional world. With her extensive expertise and dynamic approach, Mrs. Ravihari guides students through the crucial aspects of soft skills that go hand-in-hand with technical expertise to shape well-rounded professionals.

Mr. Ping's Noodles

The food fest was a refreshing experience where we set up our own stalls and sold food to university students and staff, providing a welcome break from routine activities. Unlike many others, our group prepared the food ourselves, learning valuable lessons in cooking, budgeting, maintaining hygiene, and managing event standards. It also gave us a chance to get creative with stall designs, use marketing strategies to attract customers, and gain hands-on experience in event management. Overall, it was a highly rewarding and enjoyable event.

Ping's Noodles wasn't just a food stall—it was an experience, a celebration of culture, teamwork, and entrepreneurial spirit.

At the Food Fest, our team introduced Ping's Noodles, a creative stall inspired by a Kung Fu Panda theme. With a playful Panda Mascot to attract attention, we combined engaging marketing strategies, eye-catching designs, and social media buzz to make our stall a standout. Preparing the food ourselves taught us valuable skills in cooking, budgeting, hygiene, and event management. The experience blended creativity, teamwork, and entrepreneurial spirit, making it both rewarding and unforgettable.
