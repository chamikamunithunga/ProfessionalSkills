MEETINGS

Professional Skills Module Conducted by Mrs. Ishari Ravihari

The Professional Skills module, led by the esteemed Mrs. Ishari Ravihari, offers an insightful and engaging experience aimed at empowering individuals with the essential skills for success in today's fast-paced professional world. With her extensive expertise and dynamic approach, Mrs. Ravihari guides students through the crucial aspects of soft skills that go hand-in-hand with technical expertise to shape well-rounded professionals.

In this session we learned key factors about meetings. Also terminology used when conducting a meeting. Chairing a meeting involves guiding the discussion, ensuring all participants contribute, keeping the agenda on track, managing time effectively, and summarizing key points and decisions for clarity and follow-up.

Reflections/Feedback

Learning how to chair a meeting and understanding meeting-related terminology was incredibly useful. The group activity we participated in offered valuable insights into conducting and managing meetings effectively.
